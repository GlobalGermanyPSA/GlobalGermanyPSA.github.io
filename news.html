Should I Travel During COVID-19?

Although Germany's Death Count is relatively low, the changes on contracting COVID-19 while travelling and inside Germany are quite high. In addition, many countries have travel destrictions making travel during this time an unecessary hassle. Even if travel restrictions and quarantine laws are lifted, travelling internationally or even nationally is not reccomended; especially to counties with a high number of cases and or deaths. The reintroduciton of different COVID-19 strains internationally wil result in the development of stronger and more destructive strains.
